About the Studio

ShadowPixel Studios is an independent game studio focused on creating immersive worlds, atmospheric storytelling, and engaging gameplay experiences.

Our goal is to craft games that combine strong visual identity with meaningful player-driven narratives, blending creativity, exploration, and emotional depth.

Studio Overview

  • Name: ShadowPixel Studios
  • Type: Independent Game Studio
  • Location: Prague, Czech Republic
  • Founded: 2026
  • Focus: Story-driven games, immersive worlds, and innovative gameplay

Our Games

Eclipse Realm

A dark fantasy RPG set in a collapsing kingdom where ancient forces awaken and destiny hangs by a thread.

Neon Drift

A high-speed racing experience set in a vibrant cyberpunk world where precision, reflexes, and style define victory.

Echoes of Titan

A narrative-driven sci-fi adventure exploring the remnants of a lost civilization and the secrets buried beneath its surface.

Forestbound

A calm exploration experience set in a living forest, where nature, discovery, and atmosphere take center stage.

Aetherfall

A story-driven fantasy adventure across floating islands, ancient ruins, and forgotten civilizations suspended above the clouds.
