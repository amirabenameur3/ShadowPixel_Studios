Eclipse Realm

A dark fantasy RPG forged in shadow and fire.

Genre: Action RPG / Dark Fantasy

Platform: PC (Steam), PlayStation, Xbox

Status: In Development

Story

In the final days of a dying kingdom, where the skies burn and the earth fractures beneath forgotten sins, a lone warrior emerges from the shadows—bound not by destiny, but by a haunting past.

Once a realm of light and prosperity, the capital now stands cursed, its towering spires swallowed by darkness and flame. Ancient forces, long sealed beneath the foundations of the world, have begun to stir once more, whispering through the ruins and corrupting everything they touch.

Drawn by visions that blur the line between memory and prophecy, you journey toward the heart of the fallen kingdom. Along the way, you will traverse shattered landscapes, abandoned strongholds, and lands where time itself seems broken.

But you are not alone.

Twisted creatures roam the ruins—once human, now consumed by the same power that threatens to engulf the world. Powerful entities guard the secrets of the past, each encounter pushing you closer to the truth… or deeper into madness.

Every choice you make carries weight.

Will you resist the darkness, or embrace it?

Will you restore what was lost, or let the world collapse into silence?

As the eclipse draws near, the fate of the realm will rest in your hands—and the line between hero and harbinger will begin to fade.

Gameplay / Core Mechanics

  • Real-time combat combining melee, magic, and tactical abilities
  • Character progression with skill trees and equipment upgrades
  • Exploration of interconnected regions with hidden paths and secrets
  • Choice-driven interactions that influence the world and narrative

Player Experience

You are a lone warrior navigating a kingdom on the brink of collapse. Every encounter tests your skill, every decision shapes your path, and every victory feels earned in a world that offers no mercy.

Key Features

  • Dark, immersive fantasy world
  • Deep combat system with multiple playstyles
  • Rich lore and environmental storytelling
  • Dynamic progression and meaningful choices

Neon Drift

Control the drift. Own the night.

Genre: Arcade Racing / Cyberpunk

Platform: PC (Steam), PlayStation, Xbox

Status: Released — Version 1.1

Story

In a city where the night never fades and neon lights never sleep, speed is more than entertainment — it’s power.

Megacorporations rule the skyline, controlling every system… except the underground racing network known as Neon Drift.

You are a rogue driver, rising through illegal street circuits where reputation is earned in milliseconds and lost in a single mistake. Every race is a message, every drift a signature — a challenge to those who believe the city can be controlled.

But the deeper you climb, the more the system begins to notice.

Drones start watching. Tracks become more dangerous. Rivals disappear.

What began as a race for dominance becomes something bigger — a fight to stay free in a city that was never meant to be escaped.

Gameplay / Core Mechanics

  • Fluid drifting system with responsive controls
  • Increasing speed intensity the longer you survive
  • Dynamic obstacles and traffic patterns
  • Score multiplier system based on drift chains and near misses

Player Experience

You are not just racing — you are flowing through the city. Every drift, every narrow escape, and every boost builds your momentum and pushes you closer to mastering the streets.

Key Features

  • Endless arcade racing mode
  • Synthwave-inspired visuals and atmosphere
  • Progressive difficulty scaling
  • Competitive high-score system

Echoes of Titan

Discover what was never meant to be found.

Genre: Sci-Fi Exploration / Adventure / Puzzle

Platform: PC (Steam), PlayStation, Xbox

Status: Released

Story

On a distant, abandoned planet, a forgotten past lies buried beneath silence.

You are part of a deep-space expedition sent to investigate unusual signals detected across the planet’s surface. What was expected to be a routine survey quickly turns into something far more complex.

Scattered across the landscape are remnants of an ancient civilization — structures that defy logic, machines that still function without explanation, and fragments of knowledge left behind.

As you explore deeper, you begin to uncover patterns, systems, and technologies that suggest this world was not simply abandoned… but deliberately left behind.

Every discovery brings you closer to understanding what happened here — and why it may have been meant to stay hidden.

Gameplay / Core Mechanics

  • Exploration-driven gameplay with environmental storytelling
  • Puzzle-solving using ancient alien technology
  • Signal tracking and decoding mechanics
  • Atmospheric navigation with limited resources

Player Experience

You are a lone explorer, sent to investigate anomalies detected beneath Titan’s surface. Isolation is constant, and every discovery deepens the mystery. The world doesn’t guide you — it challenges you to understand it.

Key Features

  • Immersive sci-fi narrative experience
  • Open-ended exploration on an alien moon
  • Dynamic sound design and ambient storytelling
  • Mystery-driven progression system

Forestbound

Follow the path. Discover the magic.

Genre: Cozy Puzzle / Adventure

Platform: PC (Steam), PlayStation, Xbox

Status: Released

Story

Drawn by whispers of a forgotten path, you enter a forest unlike any other.

Here, the trees seem to watch, the light dances between branches, and small creatures move just beyond sight. Scattered throughout the woods are traces of those who came before — travelers who followed the same quiet call.

As you wander deeper, the forest begins to reveal itself through hidden paths, subtle signs, and moments of quiet magic. Nothing is forced, nothing is explained — only discovered.

And perhaps, by the time you reach the heart of the forest, you will understand why it called you here.

Gameplay / Core Mechanics

  • Light puzzle-solving integrated into exploration
  • Interaction with magical creatures and forest spirits
  • Unlocking hidden paths and secret areas
  • Relaxed, non-combat progression

Player Experience

You are a quiet traveler drawn into a mystical forest where every corner holds a small wonder. There is no rush — only the joy of discovery, connection, and unraveling the forest’s gentle secrets.

Key Features

  • Cozy and relaxing gameplay experience
  • Handcrafted magical environments
  • Exploration-driven progression
  • Calm, immersive atmosphere
